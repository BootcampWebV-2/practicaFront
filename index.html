Work Experience

2012 - present day: Biodiversity Management Expert at Endesa

Endesa is one of the main electric companies in Spain. As a Biodiversity Expert, my job is to develop and monitor its Biodiversity Conservation Plan. I also report our results, both internally and to the world! I also advise the company about environmental regulation and international initiatives in topics such as Natural Capital or Ecosystem Services. I am based in the beautiful city of Lleida, but from here I manage and monitor a set of projects that span all throughout Spain!

2011 - 2012: Environmental Management Specialist at Endesa's As Pontes Thermal Power Plant

As Pontes is a thermal power plant in A Coruña (Galicia), in North-Western Spain. During my time working there as an external specialist, I prepared the environmental documentation and reports required for the renovation of its Integrated Environmental Authorisation. I did the same for its companion facility, a smaller combined-cycle power plant.

About Me

I am a curious human being who enjoys discovering new places and learning about different forms of life. This is why I became an environmental scientist in the first place.

But the Earth is not the only place I love wandering. Sometimes I like creating new worlds in my head. That's why I developed an interest in writing... and coding!

The true reason why I decided to study web development, even though it's not directly related to my current job, is because everytime I start a new project it feels like building a completely new world from scratch. I had a lot of ideas that I wanted to turn into realities, and web development is providing me with the tools I need for it.
